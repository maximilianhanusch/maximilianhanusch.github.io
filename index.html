Maximilian Hanusch, M.Sc.

Welcome to my personal webpage

I am an ecologist deeply passionate about nature and biodiversity. I currently work on the improvement of protected area networks at the Bavarian State Ministry for the Environment. Simultaneously, I'm in the final stages of my PhD in alpine ecology at Salzburg University, exploring ecological succession and ecosystem dynamics.
Thank you for visiting!

Teaching and Supervisory experience

Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy is grounded in the belief that cultivating curiosity is not just about answering questions but inspiring others to ask them. I am committed to creating a learning environment where questions are encouraged. Be it at field trips, lab sessions, or theoretical discussions — the aim is to transform the learning environment into a dynamic ecosystem in which curiosity thrives.
